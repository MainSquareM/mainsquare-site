How We Build

Our product development
approach

Main Square builds and maintains its own apps. These are the pillars we follow in every release.

Flutter Cross-Platform

Our primary framework. We build apps for iOS, Android, web and desktop from a single Dart codebase — shipping faster without sacrificing quality or native feel.

  • • Dart & Flutter SDK
  • • iOS, Android, Web & Desktop
  • • Riverpod & Provider state management
  • • Hot reload & fast iteration

React Native & Expo

TypeScript-based cross-platform development with Expo. A flexible alternative we use for products where the React ecosystem and its vast library support bring the most value.

  • • TypeScript & Expo SDK
  • • React Navigation
  • • SQLite & local notifications
  • • Material Design components

Cloud & Backend

Scalable backend infrastructure with Firebase, Supabase, and NestJS. Real-time data, authentication, and storage — chosen to match each product's needs.

  • • Firebase Auth, Firestore & Storage
  • • Supabase & real-time sync
  • • NestJS & Prisma ORM
  • • Redis & Socket.io

Product Design & UX

User-centered design that prioritizes simplicity and functionality. We build interfaces that are clean, intuitive, and refined through iteration.

  • • Clean, minimal interfaces
  • • Privacy by design
  • • Accessibility-aware development
  • • Iterative refinement
