Who We Are

Turning complex concepts into intuitive digital experiences.

Main Square is an independent software development studio headquartered in Lisbon, Portugal. We specialize in creating native mobile solutions for the iOS and Android ecosystems.

Focused on the intersection of utility and technology, we are dedicated to transforming complex concepts into intuitive, secure, and efficient digital experiences. With a minimalist design philosophy and robust software architecture, Main Square prioritizes user privacy and practical functionality above all else.

🔒

Privacy First

User data protection is at the core of every product we build. We collect only what's essential and keep everything secure.

✨

Minimalist Design

Clean interfaces, thoughtful interactions, and nothing unnecessary. Every element has a purpose.

⚡

Robust Architecture

Built on solid foundations with native technologies to ensure performance, reliability, and long-term maintainability.
